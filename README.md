# About Hiwonder

Hiwonder builds open-source hardware and software platforms for robotics research and education. Open source is in our DNA, driving everything we create, from ROS-based robots and robotic arms to quadruped and humanoid robots.

Our mission is to lower the barrier to physical robotics and embodied AI, making it easier for researchers, developers, educators, and makers to build, train, and deploy intelligent robots. Our robotic arms, including the **SO-ARM101** and **NexArm**, are natively integrated with the LeRobot ecosystem, enabling end-to-end imitation learning and reinforcement learning workflows. By pairing leader-follower teleoperation with a dual-camera setup featuring a gripper camera and an external camera, these arms can capture rich vision-action datasets, providing a solid foundation for training **Vision-Language-Action (VLA)** models.

Hardware is just the beginning. What truly matters is what the community builds with it. We believe open collaboration accelerates innovation in robotics. Whether you'd like to share datasets, contribute models, refine documentation, or build interactive demos on Hugging Face Spaces, we'd love to have you join us!
